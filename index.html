Major Health Problems

Heart Attack

Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, dizziness, nausea.

Treatment: Immediate medical attention, CPR, angioplasty, medication, surgery.

What to Do: Call emergency services immediately, remain calm and still.

What Not to Do: Do not attempt to drive yourself to the hospital.

Depression

Symptoms: Persistent sadness, fatigue, lack of interest in daily activities.

Treatment: Therapy (CBT), antidepressants, support groups, lifestyle changes.

What to Do: Seek professional help, stay active, and reach out to loved ones.

What Not to Do: Do not isolate yourself, avoid alcohol or drugs as coping mechanisms.

Anxiety

Symptoms: Restlessness, rapid heart rate, trouble concentrating, excessive worry.

Treatment: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), medication (SSRIs), relaxation techniques.

What to Do: Practice deep breathing, stay active, and try meditation.

What Not to Do: Avoid excessive caffeine, do not avoid social interactions.

Antimicrobial Resistance

Symptoms: Uncontrolled infections, recurring infections, fever, chills.

Treatment: Alternative antibiotics, infection control measures, proper antibiotic use.

What to Do: Always finish your prescribed antibiotic course, never self-medicate.

What Not to Do: Do not misuse antibiotics or take them unnecessarily.

Vector-borne Diseases

Symptoms: Fever, fatigue, headache, rashes, muscle aches.

Treatment: Disease-specific treatments (e.g., antimalarials for malaria), fluids, rest.

What to Do: Use insect repellent, wear protective clothing, and use mosquito nets.

What Not to Do: Avoid traveling to regions with high disease prevalence without proper protection.

Drug Addiction

Symptoms: Intense cravings, withdrawal symptoms, mood swings, neglecting responsibilities.

Treatment: Detox, rehabilitation programs, counseling, medications for withdrawal symptoms.

What to Do: Seek professional help, join support groups, maintain a healthy lifestyle.

What Not to Do: Do not attempt to quit cold turkey without supervision.

Stroke

Symptoms: Sudden numbness, confusion, difficulty speaking, severe headache.

Treatment: Immediate medical care, clot-busting medications, rehabilitation.

Diabetes

Symptoms: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, blurred vision, fatigue.

Treatment: Insulin therapy, oral medications, healthy diet, regular exercise.

What to Do: Monitor blood sugar levels, maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly.

What Not to Do: Avoid sugary foods, neglect regular check-ups.
